LOTTERY GAME

The rules are as follow:

1. Enter three numbers (ranging from 1 to 10).

2. Each time the game starts, three winning numbers (ranging from 1 to 10) will be randomly generated without repetition.

3. When you enter three numbers and press the draw button, the input numbers will be compared with the winning numbers (regardless of the order of arrangement).

4. If all numbers match, it is the first prize. If two numbers match, it is the second prize. If one number matches, it is the third prize.
